About me

CLAUDIA LUNA FUENTES

(MEXICO CITY, 1988)

Semblanza

Claudia Luna's work questions the ambivalence between stay and transit in public spaces, as well as the public space, as well as the structures of urbanism that provide or prevent them. impossible. In the process, she alters and inverts the perception of the regulatory objects of public architecture by by generating playful replicas, soft and colourful doubles which, being made of cardboard, are cardboard makes them flimsy and transportable, producing dynamics which are a symbolic dynamics that are a symbolic reclamation of his transient body.

Through drawings, paintings and object-paintings,

"Claudia presents urban architecture traversed by the wear and tear of time and the alteration of the human footprint."

Among the materials with which the artist experiments are paper and cardboard, supports with which she explores architecture as a scenario -or skeleton- of catastrophe and dehumanization, a consequence of decadent urbanism. Both her structures and her construction fragments are a reflection on the difficulties of the modern city.
